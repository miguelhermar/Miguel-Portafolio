About me

Hello! I am Ruchi Bhatia, a student from K.J. Somaiya College of Engineering with a B.Tech in Computer Engineering. The four-year undergrad program has introduced me to a plethora of courses in the field of Computer Engineering ranging from areas like Data Structures and Analysis of Algorithms to Computer Networks and Software Engineering.

I gravitate more towards subjects such as Data Warehouse Mining and Artificial Intelligence and also opted for an interdisciplinary course on Blockchain technology. This course excited me as it involved incorporating IT solutions to solve diverse business problems.

I started coding at an early age and my interest in this field has only grown with time. I realised that my key interests lay solely in sharing my technical expertise with people. I thoroughly enjoy tackling problems pertaining to technology and coming up with novel ideas.
